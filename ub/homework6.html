Homework 6 - Who can help

The final page you'll be adding to your website will be about accomplishing your goals. In this homework, brainstorm two things:

  1. Qualities of your "ideal" mentor to help you with your goals:
    • Are they an expert in something in particular?
    • Are they your age or older?
    • What kind of personality do they have?
    • Are they a friend or an acquaintance?
  2. At least one person in your life who has some of these qualities. They might be:
    • Teachers
    • Advisors
    • Friends
    • Family

If you can't think about who might be a good mentor for you, try listing people who you could be a good mentor for. How would you make yourself available to them? What resources could you help them gain access to?

What to Submit

Submit one page that includes answers ideas about the two topics above. This list of ideas can be handwritten or printed. Include your name on your submission.

Grading (10 points)

You'll get 5 points for describing your ideal mentor and 5 points for sharing at least one person who could be your mentor (or one person you could mentor).
